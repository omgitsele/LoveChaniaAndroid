My Crete Holiday is a tour operator that operates in the travel industry in the largest and most populated Greek island... Crete.

Founded thanks to the passion and experience of Domenico Di Liberto and Agamemnone Tsialtas, with the aim of representing a point of reference for Clients and Dealers, building customized holiday packages for them.

Thanks to the network of contacts and knowledge of the island, it can offer an extremely rich choice of structures, ready to satisfy any kind of request.

We also offer car rental services, transfers, excursions and event organization.

My Crete Holiday personally selects and controls every product and service to give you the certainty of living a safe and relaxing holiday.
