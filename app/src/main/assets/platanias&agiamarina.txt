8 km to the west of the city of Chania, is the Agia Marina beach. It merges with that of Platanias to become a long golden sandy beach, abundant attractions. Water games, partys and various animations are the main feature of these places, which attract millions of tourists every year. Are easily accessible by car or bus and are the most equipped beaches of Crete, with parking, toilets, bars, taverns, restaurants, sun loungers and parasols.
