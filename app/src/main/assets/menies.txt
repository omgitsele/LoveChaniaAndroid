At the northwest end of the peninsula Rodopou, are the two beaches of Meneies. They are immersed in the absolute quiet and surrounded by greenery. The beaches of St. George and Diktynna have emerald waters and pebbles. Accessible by boat from Kolimbari or by car (4x4), a hard dirt road, 20 km long and requires a lot of attention. The beaches are not equipped. Also frequented by nudists.
