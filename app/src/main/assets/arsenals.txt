Arsenals (in Greek Neoria) were built to the east of the port during the Venetian occupation. Between the thirteenth and seventeenth century there were 19 and were used as shipyards. During the Turkish period, 9 were destroyed and the others were used as military warehouses. Today are still visible, the big Arsenal, currently the Mediterranean Architecture Center, a group of 7 located along the quay of Akti Enoseos and 2 at the end of the port.
