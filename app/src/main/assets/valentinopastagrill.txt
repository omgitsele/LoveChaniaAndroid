From delicately flavored seafood, fine steaks, prime cuts, and real burgers, to authentic Italian pizza, pasta, and risotti, tantalizing appetizers and antipasti, Valentino Pasta & Grill restaurant near Chania, is the place to be for a leisurely or quick lunch, for a chilled dinner or on a special occasion. You may even sample several all-time Greek classics to get acquainted with the richness and goodness of our local cuisine. And if brunching is your style, here you'll find a great version, featuring a mouthwatering selection of sumptuous sandwiches, just perfect omelets, and that's not to mention Oscar & Valentino's signature mimosa breakfast.
