Is also one of the beaches of the Akrotiri peninsula, located 13km from the city of Chania. It is divided into two beaches, a small and quiet and another large, spacious and most populous. Kalathas is very popular with young people, but its shallow waters and small island that shelters the wind is ideal for families. Accessible by car or bus to the village of Chorafakia and then walk away. Kalathas is equipped for tourists and has parking, restaurants, bars, toilets, sun loungers and parasols.
