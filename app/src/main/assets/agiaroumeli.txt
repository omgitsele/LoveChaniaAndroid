Located at the end of the Samaria gorge, it takes the name of his village, small and welcoming, on the south coast of the prefecture of Chania. The beach is very long and its waters are deep and clear. Accessible by boat from Paleochora, Sougia, Loutro and Chora Sfakia or walking through the Samaria Gorge or paths west of Sougia and east of Loutro. The beach is equipped for tourists wc, bars, taverns, parasols and sun loungers. A part is also frequented by nudists.
