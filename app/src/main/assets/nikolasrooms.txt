This hotel is located in Chania Old Town and only a short distance from Firkas Fortress, Historical Archive of Crete and Nautical Museum Of Crete. The hotel is just a few steps from the Cathedral of the Presentation of the Virgin Mary, Municipal Art Gallery of Chania and Archaeological Museum of Chania.

The hotel set in the city center, the Nikolas Rooms offers easy access to Chania's sightseeing and entertainment areas.

The area around the Nikolas Rooms Hotel Chania has a lot of popular restaurants and cafes. The location allows guests to easily reach all local tourist attractions on foot.   The Chania International Airport (CHQ)  is less than a 30-minute drive away.
