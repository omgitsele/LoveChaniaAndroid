Perigiali Restaurant is located at the famous beach of Falassarna in Kissamos, Crete. Perigiali Restaurant was established 7 years ago, and has been offering fresh food, traditional Greek cuisine and quality service since then. Our staff has more than 25 years of experience in the industry. 
If you visit Falassarna Beach to relax and have an unforgettable time, we are your best option! Our Beach Service allows you to enjoy a comfortable place with sunbeds, umbrellas, great food, drinks and free Wi-Fi on one of the best beaches in Europe.
