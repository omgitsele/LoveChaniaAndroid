LET'S SPOON is a homemade italian ice cream shop, located in the heart of the old town of Chania, close to 
the Venetian harbour. The ice cream is produced with fresh and top quality products, only natural ingredients 
without any added dyes or perfumes, so you can enjoy the most healty and nutritious low fat gelato. The chocolate 
is from Domori one of the best companies in the world market. The fruity flavors are produced exclusively 
from natural fruits, without any colors or fragrances, our gelato contains up to 65% real fruits. We have selected
the Pistachio of Bronte, recognized worldwide for its sweet and aromatic flavor. Our Milkshakes and Smoothies 
are made with fresh products.
