Mitos Art has been situated in the Old Town of Chania near the Venetian Harbour since 1998.

Mitos Art represents Greek culture and expression and hosts the creations of local and renowned artists. Figurative objects of incomparable quality stem from Greek tradition and modern art.

Handmade sculptures made of bronze and aluminum, ceramics, artwork inspired by museum pieces, and modern jewellery in gold and silver are displayed in a 500-year-old renovated building of simple aesthetic.
