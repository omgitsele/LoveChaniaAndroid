This impressive monastery of the 9th century, is situated on a promontory overlooking the sea, 5 km from the stunning beach Elafonissi. Its name means "golden march" comes from an old legend which tells that one of his marches would be in gold, but would only be visible by a pure soul who believes in God.

Open every day 08:00-14:00 and 17:00-20:00
