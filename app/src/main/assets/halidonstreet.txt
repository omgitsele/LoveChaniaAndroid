It is the most populated tourist street of Chania which leads to the old Venetian harbor. Halidon unites the new and the old city starting from square 1866 and ending in Sintrivani square. In addition to the many shops along the road to the port, you will find the Cathedral of Chania and the building where once there was a Hammam turkish on the right side, the Catholic Church, the Museum of Folklore and the Archaeological Museum on the left side.
