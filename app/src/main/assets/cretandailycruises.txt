Our boats depart from the port of Kissamos (Kavonisi) for Gramvousa and Balos lagoon. Kissamos or Kasteli is a small scenic town, on the northwestern part of Crete, approximately 43 kilometers from the city of Chania. 
Gramvousa and Balos are habitats of stunning natural beauty, included in the European initiative NATURA 2000.
