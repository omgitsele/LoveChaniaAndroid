 Is located on the west coast of the island and consists of a series of large white sand beaches and turquoise waters. Today it is a place of outstanding natural beauty, with its beaches and has become one of the most famous tourist destinations of Crete. Some bars, taverns and apartments for rent are present in this place surrounded by an amazing landscape. Accessible by car or bus, Falassarna is equipped for tourists and has parking, toilets, bars, taverns, parasols and sun loungers.
