The charm of the Hammam has ancient origins that date back to Roman times, where in addition to being used to take care of their body, was also a meeting place to discuss. In the city of Chania, they were built during the Ottoman occupation, in time, through the central streets there were diverse, both public and private. The Ottomans used the Hammam daily basis for their purification. Among the main ones, there was one located Halidon Street (across from the Catholic Church) and in the streets of the historic center of Zambeliou and Douka (today a part of it, is a restaurant hall).
