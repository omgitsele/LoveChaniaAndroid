South of the prefecture of Chania, between Agia Roumeli and Loutro is Agios Pavlos, another jewel of nature, kept on the island of Crete. A long and lonely gray sand and pebble beach, washed by the blue waters of the Libyan Sea. Accessible only on foot from Agia Roumeli and Loutro, it is not equipped for tourists. It is also frequented by naturists.
