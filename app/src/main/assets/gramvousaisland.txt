The island can be reached by boat from the port of Kissamos, to take advantage of its strategic position, the Venetians fortified the island of Gramvousa in 1580 by building the castle of of Gramvousa, later occupied by the Ottomans and freed by the Cretan revolutionaries in 1825. Gramvousa was in fact the first part of Crete to be freed by the Ottoman Empire. The island became a refuge for thousands of Cretan revolutionaries and their families. The splendid view of the sea and the Gramvousa peninsula from the castle is one of the most spectacular panoramas you can find on the island of Crete.
