The Grammeno peninsula is located 5 km from the center of Paleochora. It is composed of two wonderful beaches, on its left side a beautiful beach with fine and coarse pebbles and crystal clear sea, while on the other side there is a beautiful sandy bay. The Grammeno peninsula hosts an important colony of protected junipers. Reachable by car or bus, Grammeno is equipped with umbrellas, sun beds, bars and taverns.
