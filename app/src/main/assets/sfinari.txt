The beach of Sfinari is located about 60 km west of Chania, south of the famous beach of Falassarna, can be reached by bus or by car. The village is built inside a valley. You reach Sfinari to look for tranquility, great food and direct contact with nature. There are pebbles and sand on the beach, it is not equipped but ideal for windsurfing, family vacations and snorkeling. At the end of the south beach there is the promontory of Korakas, full of carob trees. Going up the promontory in addition to admiring a fantastic panorama, you can get off the opposite side by finding the secluded beach of Platanakia.
