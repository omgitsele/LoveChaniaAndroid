 Located in northwestern peninsula Gramvusa, this charming lagoon is one of the most beautiful beaches in the world. Crystal clear waters, fine sand and its white color pink and its wild landscape that surrounds it, creating a unique spectacle! Accessible by boat from Kissamos or by car on a rough ground road after reaching the parking, you have to walk for about 20 min. Balos is equipped with bar, sunbeds and parasols only during the high season.
