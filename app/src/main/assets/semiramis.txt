In the lanes of the Old Town of Chania, within a labyrinth of myth and time, where magic blends with tradition and good taste is combined with imagination and creativity, you will find SEMIRAMIS tavern.

At Semiramis, fine traditional Greek food is accompanied by live music. To us, your taste-bud satisfaction and fun is not just a matter of mere service, but an experience of authentic hospitality!

All dishes are prepared with fresh, local ingredients, while exclusive use of virgin olive oil is made in conformity with the relevant certification by the Greek National Tourism Organisation (GNTO).
