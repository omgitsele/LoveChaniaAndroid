Strata Tours is a small company specializing in walking and activity holidays in West Crete established in 1999 by a local Greek man Stelios Mylonakis. As Stelios is a local man his knowledge and enthusiasm for his beloved area of Kisamos knows no bounds and this is combined with 30 plus years of working in various aspects of tourism. Working his way up from hotel school to run and own a bar, taverna, holiday apartments and now tourist office, Stelios has made tourism his passion and lifes' work.
